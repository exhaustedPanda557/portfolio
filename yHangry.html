yHangry

*this was a group project*

Context

My group selected a yCombinator startup and designed an interactive interface for their service. We picked yHangry, a startup that connects customers with private chefs to cook private dinner parties at their homes. We created an online platforrm for yHangry users that allows them to peruse the meal options and book.

In college, it is easy to become overwhelmed by the number and intensity of alcoholic experiences. Our work to further develop a platform for yHangry is meant to support the importance of creating more intimate and healthy non-alcholic social experiences like dinner parties.

Process

(click arrows on the slideshow for the progression)

(1) We identified what components the platform should include and what layout would be best. We created a wireframe for this.

(2): We created a hi-fi prototype and engaged in the critique process.

(3): We incorporated feedback from the critique

(4): We completed user testing, and from user's out-loud step-by-step comments, we realized the following takeaways.

Takeaway

I learned how different webpages should flow into each other and connect through buttons. Communicating lots of information across multiple webpages requires simplicity and consistency in color scheme, fonts, icons, and the navigation bar.
